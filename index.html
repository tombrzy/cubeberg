02 — Services

What I do.

End-to-end Planning Analytics — from the first whiteboard sketch to a platform your team actually enjoys using.

  • Dimensional design, cube architecture, data flows and integration blueprints. A model that mirrors how your business actually plans — and stays maintainable years later.

    Cube design · Dimensions · Data flows

  • Driver-based planning, rolling forecasts, top-down / bottom-up workflows, scenario and version management built for real planning cycles.

    Driver-based · Rolling forecast · Scenarios

  • TurboIntegrator processes, rules and feeders, TM1 REST API and TM1py automation. Robust, documented, tested — not a black box.

    TurboIntegrator · Rules & Feeders · REST API

  • TM1 to Planning Analytics, on-premises to cloud, Perspectives to PAfE. A clear migration path with no data loss and no planning downtime.

    PA on Cloud · PAW · PAfE

  • Slow cubes, memory-hungry feeders, endless TI chains — audited, profiled and fixed. Often the model doesn’t need a rebuild; it needs a specialist.

    Feeder tuning · Query performance · Audit

  • Workspace dashboards, Excel reporting with PAfE, admin coaching and hand-over. I build platforms your team can own — and stay available when it matters.

    PAW dashboards · Training · Support
